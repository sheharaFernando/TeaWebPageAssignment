DILMAH TEA

The Dilmah company was founded by MERIL J FERNANDO in 1988 and it's a Sri Lankan brand of tea sold internationally. The name of Dilmah came by combining the first names of Fernando's sons Dilhan and Malik.Dilmah tea is now sold in over 100 countries.The founder Meril Fernando was born in 1030 in Pallansena near Negombo Sri Lanka.After he moved to Colombo he became one of the Tea Testers training ate Mincing Lane,London.In 1974 he started his tea estates to to try producing Sri Lankan Tea. In 1981 he founded Ceylon Tea Services Limited.In 1985 he convinced the Australian Supermarket trailer Coles to sell his brand of Tea,Dilmah.he won the buyer about stocking his Tea.After that 35 Coles stores in Victoria and Woolworths started selling Dilmah Tea. And then Later on Dilmah Tea exported to the New Zealand and Dilmah Tea is the top selling Tea brand in New Zealand.

GEORGE STEUART TEA

Steuart Tea was founded by GEORGE STEUART in 1835.This is also embarked on a new journey to market by providing the best Sri Lankan Tea to the delight of Sri Lankan consumer.Steuart Tea is the Sri Lanka's oldest Tea exporting company.This company is originated by Captain James Steuart in 1790-1870.He was a British Sea Captain who sailed the southern seas in 1800s and in 1818 he permenantly settled down in Sri Lanka. Once he sttled down he saw the commercial potential of Sri Lanka and he set himself up as merchant adn banker.Then after that he appointed as "Master Attendant of the Roads of Colombo".With that position he gained he continued his commercial activities which he used to do.As a result of this he invited his younger brother to travel to Sri Lanka in 1808-1896 and take care of his business and in 1835 both of them established the Company naming George Steuart.In the first sight this Company manufactured Coffee and after the decrement of Coffe industry they Started manufacturing Tea using their Company.And it has won thousands of peoples' hearts because of the quality of the Tea that the have been providing.

FOUNDED - 1835
